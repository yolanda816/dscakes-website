About Us

DS is short for Delicious taste Sweet life. We used the finest ingredients to produce cakes, cookies. With the highest of standards, we are confident that with your first order and your first bite, you will become our new and loyal customer.

Custom Cakes

We are a custom cake bakery that specializes in themed custom cakes, cookies, and cupcakes for any occasion. Show us a picture for inspiration, or tell us about the theme for your party, and we can come up with a design that fits!

Cake Menu
