Time to make a cake that’s entirely you own!

Our bakery will create the perfect custom cake and cupcakes for your occasions. You can choose from our cake menu; we have many flavors & decorative options. We hand-decorate every cake. Our cake can make your magical day even more special. We can offer suggestions if you are unsure of the exact design you would like. If that’s the case, just provide some info about the celebration or celebrant to get our gears turning, and we can take it from there! Our affordable cakes range between 2 and 3 tiers depending on how small or large your event may be. We even have a teeny tiny cupcake option for COVID affected event plans.

Delivery

We can free delivery your cake when you spend $100 and up. if you need to order over the phone, please prepare the following information before you request delivery and then call the bakery. Name and address of recipient Delivery date and time A three hour delivery window is required. If the recipient is not home to receive the product during the initial agreed upon delivery window, the delivery will be returned to the bakery. Only 1 attempt is made by the driver. A 2nd attempt will require an additional delivery charge.
